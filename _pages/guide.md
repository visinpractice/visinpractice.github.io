# The VIS 2019 Practitioner Guide

{::options parse_block_html="true" /}

This guide presents a high-level guide to some of the most interesting [sessions of the IEEE VIS 2019 conference](http://ieeevis.org/year/2019/info/week-at-a-glance), with a particular emphasis on sessions that are of relevance to practitioners. For a complete list of conference sessions and the full conference program, please refere to [ieeevis.org](http://ieeevis.org/) and their [at-a-glance-schedule](http://ieeevis.org/year/2019/info/week-at-a-glance).

When preparing the practitioner guide, we incorporated the titles and abstracts of content appearing at this year's conference as well as statements elicited from paper authors. In this way, we identified six practitioner interest groups, which also appear now throughout the VIS PDF and print program available at [ieeevis.org](http://ieeevis.org/):
<span id="legend" class="anchor"></span>
* <a target="" alt="All Practitioners" title="All Practitioners" href="#ap"><span class='guide'>AP</span></a> - <a target="" alt="All Practitioners" title="All Practitioners" href="#ap">All Practitioners </a>

* <a target="" alt="Biomedical Practitioners" title="Biomedical Practitioners" href="#bp"><span class='guide'>BP</span></a> - <a target="" alt="Biomedical Practitioners" title="Biomedical Practitioners" href="#ba">Biomedical Practitioners</a>

* <a target="" alt="Data Scientists" title="Data Scientists" href="#ds"><span class='guide'>DS</span></a> - <a target="" alt="Data Scientists" title="Data Scientists" href="#ds">Data Scientists</a>

* <a target="" alt="User Experience Practitioners" title="User Experience Practitioners" href="#ux"><span class='guide'>UX</span></a> - <a target="" alt="User Experience Practitioners" title="User Experience Practitioners" href="#ux">User Experience Practitioners</a>

* <a target="" alt="Data Journalists + Science Communicators" title="Data Journalists + Science Communicators" href="#dj"><span class='guide'>DJ</span></a> - <a target="" alt="Data Journalists + Science Communicators" title="Data Journalists + Science Communicators" href="#dj">Data Journalists + Science Communicators</a>

* <a target="" alt="Simulation Scientists" title="Simulation Scientists" href="#ss"><span class='guide'>SS</span></a> - <a target="" alt="Simulation Scientists" title="Simulation Scientists" href="#ss">Simulation Scientists</a>
